What is Kapino?

Kapino is a buy and sell website purely dedicated to coffee farmers, distributors, and enthusiasts alike in the Philippines.
With Kapino, farmers are provided with a vibrant and friendly market where they get the opportunity to fulfill the demand of the buyers by simply posting the variety of beans produced in their coffee farms. Thus, giving the buyers the opportunity to find coffee products at their own convenience.
